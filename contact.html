Acquisitions & Inquiries

A dedicated channel for collectors, institutions, and researchers. We can provide dossiers (images, specs, provenance, and condition), viewing guidance, and installation notes for sculpture, works on paper, and AV works.

Acquisitions Research Press

What to include

  • Work title and year (or a link to the item on this site)
  • Intended context (private collection, institutional collection, exhibition)
  • Preferred timeline and any logistical notes (shipping, installation constraints)
  • If requesting a dossier: specify image needs (overall, details, verso), and any forms required

Typical response: 1–3 business days. For time-sensitive requests, please include “URGENT” in the subject line.

Frequently asked

Pricing and availability

Pricing is provided upon request. Some works are reserved or under review. If you are interested in a specific piece, please reference its title and year.

Condition and conservation

Condition summaries are included in dossiers. Full reports with conservation notes and high-resolution images can be prepared for institutional review.

Viewing

Studio viewings and virtual walkthroughs can be arranged by appointment. Installation views for select works are available in the Materials section.

Loan requests

For exhibition loans, include venue, dates, loan conditions, and insurance certificate. Lead time of 6–10 weeks is preferred.
