Synopsis

At the work’s center is a measured return to the image—a face that doesn’t quite resolve, water that is more structure than surface. The piece is paced to reward quiet attention and re-viewing.

When installing, the image should be calibrated for a matte, neutral field. Sound is integral; playback should avoid compression artifacts and ensure consistent stereo imaging.
